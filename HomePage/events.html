Fundraising - Future of investment in FBOs and NPOs

Funding sources of Faith Based Organisations(FBOs) and Non Profit Organisations (NPOs) have been hit hard with the pandemic and the majority are gearing up to diversify their sources and bases. Understanding of new fundraising approaches, investment landscapes and recruiting top notch strategies to fundraise becomes an essential mechanisms to uphold organizational plans and achieve goals. Most FBOs and NPOs are already undertaking multiple fundraising activities and have adequate resource base. Ability to mobilise stakeholders to start, diversify or continue investing into organisational plans is the most important aspect of organisational fundraising strategies that you will learn in this workshop.
This is a master class workshop to all leaders in non profit and faith based organisations to understand successful fundraising planning, strategies and developing roadmap. At the end of the workshop, participants will be able to develop fundraising plan, strategy and able to mainstream fundraising plan into organisational plan. Participants are invited to bring their fundraising plans for opinion.

Reducing stress, increasing productivity

Prioritizing employee wellness is pertinent part of profitability and sustainability in the contemporary business environment. Strong wellness program reduces absenteeism rate and create a positive work culture that benefits employees and organisation. Both, studies and experience suggests that investing in employee wellness can boost productivity, improve morale and commitment to serve short and long term company wellbeing.
The four hour forum is organised to demonstrate a link between wellness, profitability and work environment. The forum brings together industry leaders, wellness and worksplace experts to discuss and share strategies that reverse effects of stress into the organisation development.
